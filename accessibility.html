Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Commitment

golnoosh.nooshline.nl aims to provide a clear, accessible profile that works across desktop, tablet and mobile devices and remains usable with keyboard navigation, semantic HTML and readable contrast.

Current Measures

  • Semantic page landmarks, headings and navigation labels.
  • Responsive layout for mobile and desktop screens.
  • Visible focus states, skip link and reduced-motion support.
  • Alternative text for profile and supporting imagery.

Known Limitations

External services linked from this website, including LinkedIn and project reference websites, are controlled by their respective providers and may have different accessibility behavior.

Feedback

If you find an accessibility problem on this website, contact Golnoosh through the public channels linked from the homepage. Include the page URL, device/browser context and a short description of the issue.
